Webinars


Off the back of the success of our in person Share Club events, I led the roll out of our online webinar series to expand our reach nationally. It is a great opportunity to engage with more investors across the country. A lot of feedback we receive from our events is that they really enjoy meeting the people behind the brand, so it was a no brainer to have our Sharesies Country Manager Brendan and Co-Founder Brooke on our first investing 101 webinar. Since our first webinar we have had special guests like Cameron Gleeson (BetaShares’ Senior Investment Strategist) and Karen Trau (Perpetual’s Senior Product Manager, Listed and Direct) to chat about ethical investing. Plus Scott Phillips (The Motley Fool’s Chief Investment Officer) for our 2022 wrap up.
