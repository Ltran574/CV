Sharesies Share Club Sydney


May 2022 - We hosted our first in-person Share Club event for Sharesies investors, featuring a panel discussion with Scott Phillips (The Motley Fool AU), Queenie Tan (Invest with Queenie), and Brendan Doggett (Sharesies).

The purpose of Share Club is to help build a community of Sharesies investors and create a space for open money discussions.

I organised the content and ran the event in the lead up and on the night. Liased with talent, suppliers and sourced partners to support the night with great prizes from our friends (Pedestrian TV, SOTM, Adrian William & Frucor Suntory). Feedback from the night showed that attending the event encouraged investors to refer Sharesies to their friends and helped them feel more confident to invest with Sharesies.
