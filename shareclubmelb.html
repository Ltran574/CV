Sharesies Share Club Melbourne


August 2022 - Sharesies AU's first interstate event was in Melbourne. We had a stellar panel, featuring Victoria Devine (She's On the Money), James Kirby (Wealth Editor of The Australian), and Brendan Doggett (Sharesies).

The purpose of Share Club is to help build a community of Sharesies investors and create a space for open money discussions.

This Share Club was the first time we launched Club House - a marketplace concept where Sharesies can engage with investors before and after the panel discussion. For our first Club House activation, our product team organised mini market research activities for our investors to inform our product strategy.
